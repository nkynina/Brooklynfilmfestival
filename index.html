About the Festival 2017



See you in 2017 in Brooklyn, in New York!

This year’s Festival opens at a time of almost unprecedented global change. Seismic shifts in the USA mean we are working to explore and shape new opportunities and relationships with countries around the globe.What a delight then, to welcome some of the most thrilling storytellers from those countries, giving us a window in to their worlds and to be enthralled, provoked, moved and entertained by the tales they tell.

We love to watch and engage with the extraordinary conversations that the Festival brings to our doorstep with every edition. Brooklyn, New York has a big heart and this year, with screenings of 242 feature films from 67 countries, we are again reminded of the generosity and freedom of this awesome capital city of ours which so readily embraces this multiplicity of cultures and new voices.
